Setting up your AppFlowy account enables cloud sync, collaboration, and AI features across all your devices. This guide covers account creation, profile customization, and essential settings.

## Creating an Account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Launch AppFlowy">
        Open AppFlowy on your computer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Cloud Workspace">
        On the welcome screen, select **Cloud Workspace** to enable sync and collaboration.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign up">
        Choose your sign-up method:

        <Tabs>
          <Tab title="Email">
            1. Click **Sign up with Email**
            2. Enter your email address
            3. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
            4. Click **Create Account**
            5. Verify your email (check inbox/spam)
          </Tab>
          <Tab title="Google">
            1. Click **Continue with Google**
            2. Select your Google account
            3. Authorize AppFlowy to access your account
            4. You're signed in automatically
          </Tab>
        </Tabs>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete onboarding">
        1. Choose your workspace name
        2. Set your profile picture (optional)
        3. Complete the quick tutorial
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open AppFlowy">
        Launch the AppFlowy app on your iOS or Android device.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign up">
        Tap **Create Account** and choose:

        - **Email & Password** - Enter email and create password
        - **Google Sign-In** - One-tap authentication with Google
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify email">
        If using email sign-up:
        1. Check your email inbox
        2. Tap the verification link
        3. Return to the app
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set up profile">
        1. Add your name
        2. Upload profile picture (optional)
        3. Start using AppFlowy
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  **Privacy First**: AppFlowy respects your privacy. We only collect essential data for functionality. Read our [Privacy Policy](https://appflowy.io/privacy) for details.
</Note>

## Signing In

### First Time on a New Device

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AppFlowy">
    Launch AppFlowy on your new device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Use the same method you used to create your account:
    - Email & password
    - Google Sign-In
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for sync">
    Your workspace will sync automatically. Large workspaces may take a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the sign-in screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Create a new password
6. Sign in with your new password

<Tip>
  Use a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for each service.
</Tip>

## Profile Customization

### Accessing Your Profile

To customize your profile:

1. Click your profile picture or initials in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown
3. Or navigate to **Settings** > **Account**

### Profile Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Picture" icon="image">
    Your profile picture appears in:
    - Sidebar (top-right)
    - Collaboration mentions
    - Shared page permissions
    - Member lists

    **To change your profile picture:**
    1. Click your current picture in Profile settings
    2. Choose an option:
       - **Upload Image** - Use your own photo (JPG, PNG, max 5MB)
       - **Choose Avatar** - Select from built-in avatars
       - **Remove Picture** - Display initials instead
    3. Crop and adjust if needed
    4. Click **Save**

    <Tip>
      Use a clear, professional photo for work workspaces. Fun avatars work great for personal use.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Name & Display" icon="signature">
    **Display Name**: Appears to collaborators and in mentions
    1. Enter your preferred name (first, last, or both)
    2. Changes reflect immediately across all shared pages

    **Username**: Unique identifier for your account
    - Used in @mentions
    - Cannot be changed after creation
    - Must be unique across AppFlowy
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="About Me" icon="user-pen">
    Add a short bio visible to collaborators:
    1. Click **Edit About Me**
    2. Write a brief description (max 200 characters)
    3. Include:
       - Your role or title
       - Team or department
       - Contact preferences
    4. Click **Save**

    Example:
    ```
    Product Designer @ Acme Corp
    Working on mobile apps and design systems
    Best reached via email or Slack
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Profile Banner" icon="panorama">
    Personalize your profile page with a banner:
    1. Go to **Profile** settings
    2. Click **Change Banner**
    3. Upload an image (recommended: 1200x400px)
    4. Or choose from gradient presets
    5. Click **Save**

    Banners display when others view your profile in collaborative settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## General Settings

### Language & Region

Customize localization settings:

1. Open **Settings** > **General**
2. Configure:

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Language** | 20+ languages | Interface language |
| **Date format** | MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, etc. | How dates display |
| **Time format** | 12-hour, 24-hour | Clock format |
| **Week starts on** | Sunday, Monday | Calendar and date picker |
| **Timezone** | Auto or manual | Accurate timestamps |

<Note>
  **Sync settings across devices**: With cloud workspaces, your language, date format, and week start preferences sync automatically across all devices.
</Note>

### Appearance

Personalize the look and feel:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme">
    Choose your visual theme:
    - **Light Mode** - Bright, clean interface
    - **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes, battery-friendly
    - **System** - Auto-switches based on OS settings

    Toggle theme from:
    - **Settings** > **Appearance** > **Theme**
    - Or use quick toggle in top-right menu
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Font Settings">
    Customize typography:
    1. **Font Family** - Choose from available fonts:
       - Default
       - Serif
       - Mono
       - Additional fonts vary by platform
    2. **Font Size** - Adjust for readability:
       - Small (14px)
       - Medium (16px) - Default
       - Large (18px)
       - Extra Large (20px)
    3. **Line Height** - Spacing between lines
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Document Width">
    Control page layout:
    - **Full Width** - Content spans the entire page
    - **Centered** - Fixed width, centered (ideal for reading)
    - **Custom** - Set your preferred max width

    Access via **Settings** > **Appearance** > **Document Width**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Notifications

Manage how you receive notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open notification settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure notification types">
    Enable or disable:
    - **Mentions** - When someone @mentions you
    - **Comments** - Comments on shared pages
    - **Page shares** - New pages shared with you
    - **Workspace invites** - Invitations to workspaces
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose delivery method">
    - **In-app** - Notification center within AppFlowy
    - **Email** - Email notifications (can set frequency)
    - **Push** - Mobile push notifications
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Adjust email frequency to **Daily Digest** to reduce inbox clutter while staying informed.
</Tip>

## Account Management

### Email & Password

#### Change Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open account settings">
    **Settings** > **Account** > **Email**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter new email">
    Type your new email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify change">
    1. Click **Update Email**
    2. Verify your current password
    3. Check both old and new email for confirmation
    4. Click verification link in new email
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Change Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to password settings">
    **Settings** > **Account** > **Password**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update password">
    1. Enter current password
    2. Enter new password (min 8 characters)
    3. Confirm new password
    4. Click **Change Password**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-authenticate">
    You'll be signed out on all devices. Sign back in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Subscription & Billing

AppFlowy offers both free and paid plans:

- **Free Plan**: Unlimited pages, 1GB cloud storage, basic AI features
- **Pro Plan**: Advanced AI, unlimited storage, priority support, premium features

#### View Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. View:
   - Current plan
   - Storage usage
   - Billing cycle
   - Payment method

#### Upgrade to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open subscription settings">
    **Settings** > **Subscription** > **Upgrade to Pro**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose billing cycle">
    - Monthly ($10/month)
    - Yearly ($100/year) - Save 17%
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add payment method">
    Enter credit card or use PayPal
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm subscription">
    Review and click **Subscribe**
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pro features activate immediately.

### Data & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Export Your Data" icon="download">
    Download all your workspace data:

    1. **Settings** > **Account** > **Export Data**
    2. Choose format:
       - **ZIP Archive** - Complete workspace backup
       - **Markdown** - Plain text format
       - **JSON** - Structured data
    3. Click **Export**
    4. Download will begin automatically

    Exports include all pages, attachments, and metadata.

    See [Data Export](/advanced/data-export) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Privacy Settings" icon="shield">
    Control data sharing and analytics:

    **Settings** > **Privacy**:
    - **Usage Analytics** - Help improve AppFlowy (anonymized)
    - **Crash Reports** - Automatic error reporting
    - **AI Data Processing** - How AI features use your data

    All settings are opt-in and can be disabled anytime.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Delete Account" icon="trash">
    Permanently delete your account:

    <Warning>
      **This action is irreversible**. All your workspaces, pages, and data will be permanently deleted.
    </Warning>

    1. **Settings** > **Account** > **Delete Account**
    2. Read the warnings carefully
    3. Type your email to confirm
    4. Enter your password
    5. Click **Permanently Delete Account**
    6. You'll receive a confirmation email

    Account deletion takes effect after 30 days. You can cancel anytime during this period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Multi-Device Setup

### Syncing Across Devices

With a cloud workspace, your data syncs automatically:

1. **Install AppFlowy** on your other devices
2. **Sign in** with the same account
3. **Wait for sync** - First sync may take a few minutes
4. **Start working** - Changes sync in real-time

### Checking Sync Status

- **Desktop**: Click the sync icon in the bottom-left corner
- **Mobile**: Pull down on any page to refresh
- **View sync log**: **Settings** > **Sync** > **Sync History**

### Troubleshooting Sync

If sync isn't working:

1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure you're signed into the same account
3. Force sync: **Settings** > **Sync** > **Force Sync Now**
4. Check sync log for errors
5. Contact support if issues persist

See [Mobile Sync](/mobile/sync) for platform-specific guidance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use AppFlowy without an account?">
    Yes! Create a **Vault Workspace** to use AppFlowy completely offline without an account. However, you won't have:
    - Cloud sync
    - Collaboration features
    - Cloud AI (local AI still works)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I switch from Vault to Cloud Workspace?">
    Yes. Export your Vault Workspace as a ZIP, create a Cloud Workspace, and import the ZIP file. See [Vault Workspace](/advanced/vault-workspace) for instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data encrypted?">
    Yes. AppFlowy uses:
    - **End-to-end encryption** for cloud data
    - **TLS/SSL** for data in transit
    - **Encrypted storage** on our servers

    Even we cannot access your content. Learn more in our [Security docs](/self-hosting/security).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I downgrade from Pro to Free?">
    - You keep all existing data
    - Advanced AI features become unavailable
    - Storage limited to 1GB (existing data remains accessible)
    - Premium colors and options revert to default

    No data is deleted when downgrading.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
