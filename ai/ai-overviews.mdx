## Overview

AI Overviews provide Google-style AI-generated summaries that answer your questions based on your entire workspace content. Instead of browsing through multiple pages, get a comprehensive answer with citations to source pages.

<Info>
  AI Overviews were introduced in v0.9.3 and are inspired by Google's AI Overview feature, bringing intelligent summarization to your workspace.
</Info>

## How AI Overviews Work

When you ask a question, AI Overviews:

1. **Search** your workspace for relevant pages
2. **Analyze** the content to understand context
3. **Generate** a comprehensive summary
4. **Cite** source pages with direct links
5. **Highlight** key points and important details

## Accessing AI Overviews

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` to open the search panel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ask a Question">
    Type a natural language question instead of just keywords:
    - "What are the main features of our Q1 release?"
    - "Summarize the discussion from yesterday's team meeting"
    - "What decisions were made about the redesign?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View AI Overview">
    The AI Overview appears with:
    - A detailed answer to your question
    - Highlighted key themes and important details
    - Source links to pages used for the answer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Sources">
    Click on source links to read the full context from specific pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Press `Cmd/Ctrl + ↓` while in search to toggle between regular search results and AI Overview mode.
</Tip>

## AI Overview Components

### The Answer Section

The main answer provides:

- **Comprehensive response** - Detailed answer synthesizing information from multiple pages
- **Clear structure** - Organized into logical sections
- **Direct addressing** - Answers specifically to your question
- **Contextual details** - Includes relevant dates, names, and specifics

### Highlights Section

Key themes presented as a markdown bullet list:

- **Main points** - Core themes from your content
- **Important details** - Critical information like dates, locations, decisions
- **Action items** - Tasks or next steps mentioned
- **Key metrics** - Numbers, deadlines, or quantitative data

### Sources Section

Links to pages used to generate the overview:

- **Page titles** - Clear identification of source documents
- **Clickable links** - Direct navigation to full pages
- **Source validation** - Verify the AI's answer against original content
- **Further reading** - Explore topics in more depth

## Writing Effective Questions

### Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Specific">
    ✅ "What are the main features planned for the Q1 release?"
    
    ❌ "features"
    
    Specific questions get more focused, useful answers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Ask Complete Questions">
    ✅ "What decisions were made about the pricing model?"
    
    ❌ "pricing"
    
    Complete questions help the AI understand what information you need.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Include Context">
    ✅ "What feedback did users give about the beta release last month?"
    
    ❌ "feedback"
    
    Adding context (time, topic, scope) helps narrow down relevant content.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Action Words">
    ✅ "Summarize the main points from the strategy document"
    ✅ "Compare the two design proposals"
    ✅ "List all action items from team meetings"
    
    Action words (summarize, compare, list, explain) guide the AI's response format.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Advanced Features

### Context-Aware Summaries

AI Overviews understand:

- **Temporal context** - "last week", "Q1", "recent" are interpreted correctly
- **Project context** - Recognizes project names and team structures
- **Priority levels** - Understands "urgent", "critical", "low priority"
- **Status indicators** - Recognizes "completed", "in progress", "blocked"

### Multi-Source Synthesis

The AI combines information from multiple pages:

- **Cross-references** - Connects related information across pages
- **Timeline building** - Orders events chronologically
- **Theme identification** - Finds common themes across documents
- **Contradiction handling** - Notes when sources provide different information

### Intelligent Source Selection

The AI prioritizes:

- **Recent content** - Newer pages for time-sensitive queries
- **Complete information** - Pages with comprehensive content
- **Authoritative sources** - Official documentation over casual notes
- **Relevant sections** - Specific parts of long documents

<Note>
  AI Overviews may not cite every page in your workspace. The AI selects the most relevant sources for your specific question.
</Note>

## Limitations and Considerations

### Content Boundaries

- AI Overviews only search **content you have access to** in your workspace
- Private pages you can't access won't be included
- Deleted or archived pages are excluded

### Answer Accuracy

<Warning>
  Always verify important information by checking the source pages. AI Overviews are summaries and may not capture every nuance.
</Warning>

- The AI synthesizes information but may miss context
- For critical decisions, review source documents directly
- Sources are provided so you can verify the answer

### Internet Connection Required

AI Overviews require an active internet connection:

- Cloud-based AI processing
- Real-time content analysis
- For offline AI, use **RAG Search** in Vault workspaces

## Troubleshooting

### No Overview Generated

If AI Overview doesn't appear:

1. Ensure your question is specific enough
2. Check that you have relevant content in your workspace
3. Try rephrasing as a complete question
4. Verify your internet connection

### Incomplete or Vague Answers

- Add more detail to your question
- Include time ranges, project names, or specific topics
- Check if source pages have enough information
- Try breaking complex questions into smaller parts

### Wrong or Irrelevant Sources

- Refine your question with more specific terms
- Add context about what you're looking for
- Use filters to limit search scope
- Review and improve your page titles and content for better searchability
