## Overview

AppFlowy offers home screen widgets for both iOS and Android, giving you instant access to your most important pages without opening the app. Widgets update automatically to show your latest content.

<Info>
  Widgets are available on iOS (v0.9.8+) and Android (v0.9.9+). Make sure you're running the latest version of AppFlowy.
</Info>

## Available Widgets

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Recent Pages" icon="clock">

  <Card title="Favorites" icon="star">

## Adding Widgets on iOS

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter edit mode">
    Long-press on an empty area of your iOS home screen until the apps start jiggling.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add widget">
    Tap the **+** button in the top-left corner of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find AppFlowy">
    Scroll down or search for **AppFlowy** in the widget gallery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose widget type">
    Swipe left and right to see the available widgets:
    - **Recent Pages**: Shows recently viewed pages
    - **Favorites**: Shows favorited pages  
    - **Quick Page Access**: Direct link to a specific page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select size">
    Choose your preferred widget size (Small, Medium, or Large). Different sizes show different amounts of content.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to home screen">
    Tap **Add Widget** to place it on your home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position the widget">
    Drag the widget to your preferred location and tap **Done**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Configuring iOS Widgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press the widget">
    Long-press the AppFlowy widget on your home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Edit Widget">
    Tap **Edit Widget** from the context menu.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    For **Quick Page Access** widgets, select which page the widget should open.
    
    Other widgets update automatically based on your usage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Done">
    Tap outside the widget to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding Widgets on Android

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press home screen">
    Long-press on an empty area of your Android home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Widgets">
    Tap **Widgets** from the menu that appears.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find AppFlowy">
    Scroll through the widget list or search for **AppFlowy**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose widget type">
    Select the widget you want to add:
    - **Recent Pages**: Shows recently viewed pages
    - **Favorites**: Shows favorited pages
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drag to home screen">
    Long-press the widget and drag it to your preferred location on the home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resize (optional)">
    Release the widget and use the resize handles to adjust its size.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Configuring Android Widgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press the widget">
    Long-press the AppFlowy widget on your home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap settings icon">
    Tap the settings/gear icon that appears.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure options">
    Adjust available settings for the widget. Options vary by widget type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Tap outside the widget or press **Done** to save changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Widget Sizes & Content

### iOS Widget Sizes

| Size | Recent Pages | Favorites | Quick Page Access |
|------|--------------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Small** | 2 pages | 2 pages | 1 page icon |
| **Medium** | 4 pages | 4 pages | 1 page with title |
| **Large** | 8 pages | 8 pages | 1 page with preview |

### Android Widget Sizes

Android widgets are flexible and can be resized after placement:

- **Minimum**: Shows 2-3 pages
- **Default**: Shows 4-5 pages  
- **Maximum**: Shows up to 8-10 pages depending on screen size

<Tip>
  Larger widgets show more pages but take up more home screen space. Start with a medium size and adjust based on your needs.
</Tip>

## Managing Favorites

To make the most of the Favorites widget, you need to mark pages as favorites in AppFlowy:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AppFlowy">
    Launch the AppFlowy mobile app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to a page">
    Open the page you want to favorite.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the star icon">
    Tap the star icon in the page header or use the more menu (•••).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The page is now added to your favorites and will appear in the Favorites widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can also favorite pages from the desktop app. Favorites sync across all your devices.
</Note>

## Widget Updates & Refresh

### Automatic Updates

Widgets refresh automatically when:
- You open a page in AppFlowy (updates Recent Pages)
- You add or remove favorites (updates Favorites)  
- You return to the home screen
- The system refreshes widgets (varies by platform)

### Manual Refresh

If a widget isn't showing the latest content:

**iOS:**
1. Open the AppFlowy app
2. Navigate through a few pages
3. Return to the home screen
4. The widget should update within a few seconds

**Android:**
1. Open the AppFlowy app
2. Make changes to relevant pages
3. Return to the home screen  
4. Swipe down on the widget to refresh (if supported by your launcher)

### Background Refresh (iOS)

For best widget performance on iOS:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open iOS Settings">
    Go to **Settings** on your iPhone or iPad.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find AppFlowy">
    Scroll down and tap **AppFlowy**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable Background Refresh">
    Turn on **Background App Refresh**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Widget not appearing in gallery

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're running the latest version of AppFlowy
2. Restart your device
3. Reinstall AppFlowy if the issue persists

### Widget shows "No content"

**Recent Pages:**
- Open AppFlowy and browse a few pages
- The widget will populate as you use the app

**Favorites:**
- Mark some pages as favorites in AppFlowy
- Open the app to trigger a widget refresh

**Quick Page Access (iOS):**
- Long-press the widget and tap Edit Widget
- Select a page from the list

### Widget not updating

**iOS:**
1. Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled for AppFlowy
2. Remove and re-add the widget
3. Open the AppFlowy app to trigger a refresh

**Android:**
1. Check that AppFlowy isn't restricted by battery optimization:
   - Settings > Apps > AppFlowy > Battery
   - Select **Unrestricted** or **Optimized**
2. Remove and re-add the widget
3. Restart your device

### Widget opens wrong page

**Quick Page Access (iOS):**
1. Long-press the widget
2. Tap **Edit Widget**
3. Select the correct page

**Recent Pages/Favorites:**
- This can happen if pages were deleted or moved
- Remove and re-add the widget
- Update your favorites list

### Widget looks blurry or pixelated

1. Remove the widget
2. Restart your device
3. Re-add the widget
4. If the issue persists, check for app updates

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Use multiple widgets**: Add both Recent Pages and Favorites for maximum flexibility.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Organize favorites strategically**: Keep your Favorites list curated to 5-10 most important pages for the cleanest widget experience.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Try different sizes**: Experiment with widget sizes to find what works best for your workflow and screen layout.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **iOS Quick Page Access**: Create dedicated widgets for your top 2-3 pages you access daily.
</Tip>

## Privacy & Security

- **Lock screen privacy**: Widgets can appear on your lock screen. Consider this when deciding what to favorite.
- **Widget content**: Widgets only show page titles and icons, not page content.
- **Offline access**: Widgets display cached data, so they work even when offline.

<Note>
  If your device is locked with a passcode, widgets may show limited or no information until unlocked, depending on your device settings.
</Note>
