## Overview

AppFlowy for Android brings your collaborative workspace to any Android device. Create documents, manage databases, and collaborate with your team from anywhere.

<Info>
  AppFlowy for Android requires Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or later. The app supports both phones and tablets.
</Info>

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download from Google Play Store">
    Open the Google Play Store on your Android device and search for "AppFlowy" or visit the [AppFlowy Play Store page](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.appflowy.appflowy).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Tap **Install** to download and install AppFlowy. The app is free to download.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign in or create an account">
    Launch AppFlowy and sign in with your existing account or create a new one.
    
    Sign-in options include:
    - Email and password
    - Magic link
    - One-Time Password (OTP)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start working">
    Your workspace will sync automatically. Access all your documents, databases, and shared pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  First time syncing from desktop? Read [this guide](https://docs.appflowy.io/docs/guides/sync-desktop-and-mobile) to ensure a smooth transition.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### App won't sync

1. Check your internet connection
2. Look for the network indicator in the app  
3. Ensure AppFlowy isn't restricted by battery optimization:
   - Go to Settings > Apps > AppFlowy
   - Select **Battery**
   - Choose **Unrestricted** or **Optimized** (not Restricted)
4. Try closing and reopening the app
5. Sign out and sign back in

### Keyboard won't close

1. Tap the back button
2. Tap outside the text field
3. Update to the latest version (this was fixed in v0.4.2)

### Images not loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear the image cache in Settings
3. Try closing and reopening the app
4. Ensure you have storage permission enabled

### Small screen issues

- Login page overflow was fixed in v0.9.7
- Adjust the scale factor in Settings for better readability
- Use landscape mode for wider content like tables and databases

## Performance Tips

- Keep the app updated for the latest performance improvements
- Clear cache periodically in Settings > Manage Data
- Close unused workspaces to reduce memory usage
- Use compact mode for databases to see more data on smaller screens
